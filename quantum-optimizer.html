How It Works

From raw data to autonomous action

A five-layer intelligence pipeline that ingests, learns, decides, and executes — continuously.

01
Data Ingestion
POS transactions, ERP inventory, WMS movements, and 50+ external signals (weather, events, social) stream in real-time via Kafka and REST connectors. Every data point is validated, normalised, and tagged before entering the AI pipeline.
02
AI Demand Modelling
Ensemble models (LSTM neural networks, XGBoost, and seasonal decomposition) train on your historical data and continuously retrain as new signals arrive. Forecast horizons from 1 day to 52 weeks, at SKU × location granularity.
03
Intelligent Alerting
Exception-based workflows surface only what needs human attention — stockout risks, expiry windows, overstock flags, and supplier lead-time deviations. Role-based alerts reach the right person on the right device instantly.
04
Autonomous Execution
Configurable automation rules let the system generate, approve, and transmit purchase orders directly to ERP — without human intervention. Full audit trail on every autonomous action, with override capability at any point.
05
Continuous Learning
Every forecast outcome, manual override, and market event feeds back into the model. Quantum Optimizer gets measurably more accurate every cycle — becoming a self-improving intelligence layer for your entire supply chain.
